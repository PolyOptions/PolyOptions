About

PolyOptions was founded in 2022 as a decentralized platform for crypto investors to trade short-term price movements without paying large exchange fees.

The advantage of PolyOptions is that, unlike most exchanges, users are able to execute trades without paying commissions. Trading on PolyOptions is as simple as connecting a wallet, choosing an asset, and predicting whether it will go up or down a ceratin percentage within a given time. If you're right, you get a large payout. If you're wrong, You lose a percentage of your investment. Trading with PolyOptions is that simple. No commissions. No hidden fees.
