About

PolyOptions was founded in 2022 as a decentralized platform for crypto investors to trade short-term price movements without paying large exchange fees.

The advantage of PolyOptions is that, unlike most exchanges, users are able to execute trades without paying commissions. Trading on PolyOptions is as simple as connecting a wallet, choosing an asset, and predicting whether it will go up or down a ceratin percentage within a given time. If you're right, you get a large payout. If you're wrong, you lose a percentage of your investment. Trading with PolyOptions is that simple. No commissions. No hidden fees.

FAQ

What's stopping PolyOptions from running away with the money?

PolyOptions is fully decentralized and built using smart contracts. All transactions take place within the smart contract. In addition, the project is fully open source

How is PolyOptions commission free?

An algorithm is utilized to hedge invested crypto against the predicted movement of an asset, which allows the exchange to turn a small profit on every transaction. In addition, there is a very small betting margin on some positions.

Where does the price data come from?

All currency data is sourced from cryptocurrency exchanges across the globe

What currency is supported on PolyOptions?

The only supported currency is MATIC. MATIC was chosen due to its incredibly low gas fees

What separates PolyOptions from other DeFi platforms?

The commission free nature of the platform is the biggest advantage over other platforms.
